Privacy Policy for Revi: Manga/Manhwa Reader App

Effective Date: August 5, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Revi, a Manga/Manhwa reader app. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Revi is designed with your privacy in mind and collects minimal personal information. We collect:

  1. Feedback: Information that you voluntarily provide through feedback forms in the app.
  2. Logs: Anonymous logs of your app usage from launch until feedback submission, including interactions with features. You have the option to include these logs with your feedback when you submit it. The choice to include or exclude logs is entirely up to you.

By submitting feedback and logs, you acknowledge that you have reviewed the data you provide and confirm that it does not include sensitive personal information. Any inclusion of such data is your responsibility.

We do not collect or process sensitive personal data such as health information, financial details, or biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • Implement new features based on user recommendations
  • Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any user data with third parties. Revi operates independently and does not collect user data from any external sources.

Data Storage and Security

Feedback and logs are stored anonymously and cannot be traced back to individual users. We use Supabase for data hosting, which employs its own security measures to protect stored data.

User Rights

While we do not collect personal data that can be attributed to individual users, we respect your privacy rights. If your country's laws permit, you may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Deletion of your data

Please note that due to the anonymous nature of our data collection, we may not be able to identify specific user data.

Device Permissions

Revi requires permission to read and write to your device's storage. This is necessary for features such as saving chapters and continuing from the last read chapter. You can manage these permissions through your device settings.

Age Restrictions

Revi is intended for users aged 13 and above. If you are under 13, please do not use the app or submit any information to us.

Third-Party Libraries

Revi uses various third-party libraries to provide its functionality. While we have chosen libraries that do not automatically collect data, users should be aware that the app's framework and certain features rely on external code.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes within the app. Your continued use of Revi after such modifications will constitute your acknowledgment of the modified Privacy Policy.

Legal Compliance

Revi is committed to upholding user privacy and strives to align with general privacy principles. However, privacy laws differ across jurisdictions, and we cannot ensure compliance with every local regulation globally. Users are responsible for determining if their use of Revi complies with relevant local laws.

By using Revi, you agree to this Privacy Policy and acknowledge that your use is subject to its terms. We are not liable for privacy compliance beyond the scope of this policy.
